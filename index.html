Our Story

A Brotherhood
of the Fairway

The Carnalea Nomads are a golf society rooted in the beautiful grounds of Carnalea Golf Club, nestled on the shores of Belfast Lough in Bangor, County Down.

Every Sunday we gather — friends, rivals, and gentlemen of the game — to compete, to laugh, and to share in the finest sport the world has to offer.

Twice a year we venture further afield for our celebrated Captain's Day and President's Day trips, where memories are made and legends are born.

Golf is the closest game to the game we call life. You get bad breaks from good shots; you get good breaks from bad shots — but you have to play the ball where it lies.
— Bobby Jones
