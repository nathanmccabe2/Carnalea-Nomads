Our Story

A Brotherhood
of the Fairway

The Carnalea Nomads are a golf society rooted in Carnalea Golf Club, on the shores of Belfast Lough in Bangor, County Down.

Every Sunday we gather to compete, laugh, and enjoy the finest sport in the world. Twice a year we venture afield for Captain's Day and President's Day.

Golf is the closest game to the game we call life. You get bad breaks from good shots; you get good breaks from bad shots — but you have to play the ball where it lies.
— Bobby Jones
